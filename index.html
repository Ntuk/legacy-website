Who am I?

I have been working in the restaurant industry for 13 years (2005 - 2018) and until October 2018 I was sous chef at a restaurant Aino. Thanks to my kitchen experience, I am a natural team player & my ability to endure stress is extremely high. After leaving the kitchen life behind I found myself working as IT-Support Specialist at Pagero. At Pagero We help companies streamline their business and we aim to become the largest network between businesses worldwide!

Interests

I get excited about things easily, especially about technology. My long-term interests include programming, web design, 3d modeling, user Experience, good food, martial arts and PC gaming. I have been dabbling with computers since I was a kid, from making my own text-based games to making websites for ActionQuake 2 clans and even doing some level design using Worldcraft 1.6 (some of the maps still run on the servers in 2019!).

Hobbies

I have always enjoyed PC gaming and It all started from playing Quake 2 and Diablo online. After Quake & Diablo I moved to World of Warcraft, which I dropped after I started studying at Haaga-Helia and swapped mostly to Overwatch. From the beginning of 2019 I re-started practicing Bujinkan Budo Taijutsu that I practiced in 2004-2005 and quit since after my working shifts became irregular. I train at Bujinkan Shinden Dojo Helsinki.
